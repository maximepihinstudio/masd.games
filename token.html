MASD Game Token

  • Токен TRS: Это главный жетон вознаграждения за игру.
  • Жетон позволяет: разблокировать новые карты, участвовать в игровых режимах и специальных событиях, покупать обычный расходный материал, обновлять оружие.

Token Functions

Purchase Game Items

The MASD can be used to buy in game assets, land and military equipment for the avatar.

Play to earn

Users can earn. MASD tokens through participation in different in game events such as competitions.

Vibrant Economic Incentives for Growth and Sustainability

Open market to trade and exchange NFT's as well as lucrative rewards for land owners.

Governance

Token holders could participate in the decentralized governance process that decides on the use of revenues, game development and growth.

Staking Rewards

MASD token can be used in the system to earn staking rewards. It is designed to encourage users to hold tokens and support the ecosystem.

Gamified Finance

One of our strategic visions within MASD GAMES is to create monetary incentives for the most committed and motivated players.

Staking yield

Based on our staking and reward projections, we will allocate a fair amount of tokens on staking rewards. This will incentivize stakers to hold their tokens during the early stage of the game development when some game features will still be under development.

NFT collateral

When a user buys NFT from the system, a percentage of the purchase price goes into a special collateral account associated with NFT. User then has an option to sell it back to the system (i.e. destroy the NFT) for the price equivalent to the locked collateral (or a percentage of it).

NFT Rental

NFT's, including land, can be rented to other players. The smart contract will make sure that the borrower cannot resell or destroy the object, and makes sure that the object returns to the owner when the rent period ends.

NFT Marketplace

Game items could both be traded through the in-game marketplace and also the NFT marketplaces on other blockchains. It creates liquidity for the assets created through the game and also encourages more players to participate in building the MASD world.

token F.A.Q.

What features does MASD GAMES include?

NFT's, including land, can be rented to other players with a special smart contract which will manage the renting process and requirements. Players will be able to stake their MASD tokens and get a staking yield.

STEAM or EPIC GAMES?

MASD GAMES will make it possible to play on major gaming platforms. At the moment, not all platforms make it possible to play blockchain games but we are working on it.

Playing on STEAM

Will be released on Steam for everyone to play, no wallet or blockchain skills needed. However, you will be offered to purchase various in game items to customize your avatars and virtual plots.

Do I need a crypto wallet to play?

No, you will not be asked to enter a crypto wallet to connect and participate in the game. In MASD GAMES, only you will decide how much you want to participate in the decentralized functions of the game and DeFi At the same time, if you decide to join the Community Council or purchase various game items such as NFT, you will need a cryptocurrency wallet to complete the transaction.

What is an MASD token?

MASD is an in game currency which you will use in order to purchase in game items and NFT's in the marketplace and win as a reward during competitions, and quests. What is more, owning an MASD token will enable you to stake them in return for a reward and participate in the governance process through the Community Council.
