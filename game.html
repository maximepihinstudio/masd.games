F.A.Q. by game

What is MASD Games?

MASD is a multiplayer 3D game with blockchain technology. Be the first to create your base and play to earn a MASD token, buy or rent virtual plots of land, houses, items for survival in the game. Create your own clans and communities around the world in the game to participate in competitions for valuable items and MASD tokens together. Being inspired by successful games like CS:GO and RUST, MASD.Games combines the best of both worlds - and also allows players to earn money playing their favorite game and own limited resources purchased in the form of NFT.

When will the game be released?

Early MASD investors will be invited to join the testing of Steam and Epic Games in January 2022. Over time, various game features will be added to Masd Games (you can view the roadmap for more details). We expect that the full version of the game will be released on all resources in the spring of 2022.

How do I get on board?

Players will be able to access the game through traditional gaming platforms such as Steam and Epic Games, as well as the official MASD GAMES website.

What are the key features of MASD GAMES?

MASD includes many gaming features designed to enhance the gaming experience of users. Players can trade their land and other game items, as well as rent them out, such as houses, bunkers, weapons and equipment on the virtual in-game market. Virtual islands are important elements of the gaming infrastructure. Players can buy plots of land from MASD after adaptation or on the market. It is important to note that there are few plots in the universe, and each plot of land is represented as NFT tokens Avatars are used to represent players in the game. Players can customize and personalize their avatars with combat gear and modify them by installing various assets on them. Players can collect and sell various in-game items such as houses, bunkers, weapons and equipment in the virtual in-game market. MASD GAMES will have a reputation and rating system that rewards players with various items opportunities to work on a common goal and pool resources to complete exciting tasks. For example, the game will implement community-created events in which players will participate in events and competitions. It will also include a decentralized forum where users will be able to discuss game actions, find out the schedule of upcoming votes in a decentralized organization, search for new friends, etc.
