Masd Games

MASD is a multiplayer 3D game with blockchain technology. Be the first to create your base and play to earn a MASD token, buy or rent virtual plots of land, houses, items for survival in the game. Create your own clans and communities around the world in the game to participate in competitions for valuable items and MASD tokens together.

Being inspired by successful games like CS:GO and RUST, MASD-GAMES combines the best of both worlds and also allows players to earn money playing their favorite game and own limited resources purchased in the form of NFT.

Features of the Game

Avatars

Each player is represented by an in the game, and players could with others through the avatar. could also modify the avatar by different assets to it.

Virtual Islands

In the game, players could either purchase virtual plots from MASD or the marketplace. There is a scarce of plots in the universe and each land is represented in the form of tokens.

In-Game Assets

There are in-game assets that can deployed into the game and they bought on the marketplace. Among them - houses, buy/sell NFT heroes, weapons, equipment and items.

Token

MASD token is the main currency of game, it enables players to NFTs, buy land and use specific DeFi products such as collateralization, buyback, and staking, mainly in the phases of the game development
